How to use

Step 1

Input all of the diameters of the pipes in the Pipe Information section

Step 2

Click the calculate button

Step 3

If the pipes are not wood, reduce the width until you get close to your desired length.

if you go over your desired length, increase the width.

Step 4

Increase the length until you are at your desired size.

Step 5

You can print the results or save the results to a csv file.
